Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I play a game?

Click on any game image on the homepage to start playing instantly.

2. Do I need to create an account?

No, you can play games without an account. However, an account allows you to save progress and access premium features.

3. My game is not loading. What should I do?

Try refreshing the page, clearing your browser cache, or using a different browser. If the issue persists, contact support.

4. What should I do if I forgot my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password.

5. Are the games free to play?

Yes! All our basic games are free. However, some premium features may require a subscription.

6. How can I contact customer support?

You can reach us via the contact form below, or through our email support@lootify.com.

7. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, and cryptocurrency for premium subscriptions.
